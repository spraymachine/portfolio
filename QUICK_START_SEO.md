### "My site isn't showing up in Google"
- Wait 48-72 hours after submitting to Search Console
- Check "Coverage" in Search Console for errors
- Make sure robots.txt allows crawling
- Verify sitemap is submitted

### "I'm not ranking for my name"
- Make sure LinkedIn profile is updated
- Share portfolio on social media
- Wait at least 1-2 weeks
- Create content mentioning your name

### "My Google My Business isn't verified"
- Check mail for verification postcard (takes 5-7 days)
- Try phone verification if available
- Make sure address is accurate
